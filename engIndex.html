QUALITY PRODUCTS OF VARIOUS BRANDS FROM TURKIYE

OS2 is the official distributor of well-known Turkish brands in Kazakhstan and Kyrgyzstan

We supply products of famous Turkish brands to many well-known supermarkets. Among our clients are industry giants like "Magnum", "Small", and other stores throughout Kazakhstan and Kyrgyzstan.

Carries out sales in major cities and regions, having its own warehouse and distribution network..

We own a large warehouse from which we supply throughout Kazakhstan and Kyrgyzstan.

We work with renowned brands like "Pinar", "TAMEK", and "Mutlukal Tortillas".

Among the brands we work with are some of the leaders in the Turkish segment of the food industry, true giants. The quality of such products has no analogs in Central Asia. We are expanding, and the number of brands we work with is growing.
